Burrito

Tacos are fantastic, but a good burrito will feed you for a few days. That said burritos can come in alot of sizes, whether they are the size of a paper towel roll ( bad example) to t he size of a small child. They can also be filled with some wonderful and amazing things, just like the taco. lets get into some tasty burritos

Mission Burrito

There is the mission style Burrito, which will be packed full of rice beans, and plenty of meat. This is one that I especially like when I am going out for a early dinner, I know im not gonna need anything else for a long while. A real stick to the bones style burrito.

The Breakfat Burrito

The breakfast burrito is probably one of the most recognizable types of burrito. You can never go wrong with a good breakfast burrito, whether in a retaurant or microwaving one before work. Something about the combination of cheese, sausage, bacon, jalepenos, a nice helping of queso, and some sour cream its such a great way to start your day!
